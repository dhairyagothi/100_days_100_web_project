Missionaries and Cannibals Game

How to Play

Your goal is to get all the missionaries and cannibals across the river safely.

Rules:

  • The boat can carry a maximum of 2 people at a time.
  • If cannibals outnumber missionaries on either bank, the missionaries will be eaten.

Steps:

  1. Select one or two passengers to board the boat from the current bank.
  2. Move the boat to the opposite bank.
  3. Unload the passengers on the other bank.
  4. Ensure that no missionaries are outnumbered by cannibals on either bank.
  5. Repeat until all missionaries and cannibals are on the right bank.
  6. Click on M or C to move them from one place to another,
