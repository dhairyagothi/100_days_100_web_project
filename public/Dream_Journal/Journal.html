TYPES

NIGHTMARE

DREAM

A nightmare is a distressing dream that stirs up strong feelings of fear, dread, or anxiety. Unlike regular dreams, nightmares are often vivid and can wake you up with a racing heart, lingering unease, and a sense of impending doom. By keeping a dream journal, you can start to identify patterns and triggers for your nightmares, helping you understand and eventually overcome them. Hope that this is helpful!

PEACEFUL

DREAM

Peaceful dreams are the gentle whispers of our subconscious, offering tranquility and comfort. These dreams transport you to serene landscapes, soothing scenarios, and bring a sense of calm. They leave you feeling refreshed and uplifted, like a warm hug for your soul

ADVENTURE

DREAM

Adventure dreams are thrilling escapades into the unknown. They whisk you away to uncharted territories and daring quests, whether it's exploring ancient ruins, sailing stormy seas, or battling mythical creatures. These dreams ignite your sense of wonder and excitement, leaving you with a spirit of adventure upon waking.
