Game Rules

  • Active player is indicated by a reddish-orange background
  • Rolling a 1 ends your turn and passes to the next player
  • Pressing HOLD adds current score to total and passes turn
  • First player to reach 100 points wins
  • Players can roll as many times as they want before holding
  • Game ends when a player reaches 100 points
